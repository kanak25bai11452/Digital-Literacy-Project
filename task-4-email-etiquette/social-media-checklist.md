# Social Media Do's and Don'ts

## ✅ Do's
- Maintain a professional profile  
- Share positive and meaningful content  
- Respect others' opinions  
- Use privacy settings wisely  
- Think before posting  

## ❌ Don'ts
- Don't share personal or sensitive information  
- Don't post offensive or inappropriate content  
- Don't spread fake news  
- Don't engage in online arguments  
- Don't use unprofessional language
