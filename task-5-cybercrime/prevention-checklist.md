# Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist

## General Safety Tips
- Always verify links before clicking  
- Do not share personal or banking information  
- Use strong and unique passwords  
- Enable two-factor authentication  
- Avoid using public Wi-Fi for transactions  
- Keep your apps and devices updated  

## UPI & Financial Safety
- Never share your UPI PIN with anyone  
- Do not scan unknown QR codes to receive money  
- Always check “Pay” vs “Collect” requests carefully  
- Use only official banking or payment apps  

## Reporting Cybercrime
- National Cyber Crime Portal: https://cybercrime.gov.in  
- Helpline Number: 1930
